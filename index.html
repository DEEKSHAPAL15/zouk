Zouk is a 100% Vegan & Proudly Indian brand. We make laptop bags, handbags, women's office bags, female wallets, mini wallets, sling bags, bucket bags, backpacks, travel pouches for women & men. We use Ikat fabric

homemakers and college students. Many people also choose our products for gifts in Weddings, Anniversary, Diwali, Holi, Christmas, New Year, Karwa Chauth, Valentine's Day, Women's Day. The company was founded by
